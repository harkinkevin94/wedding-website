Getting Around in China

Taxis

Taxis can be hailed on the street, at taxi stands, or through mobile apps like DiDi Chuxing. Make sure to have the address written in Chinese.

Useful Apps: DiDi Chuxing

Subways

Most major cities have efficient subway systems. Maps and signs are often bilingual.

Recommended App: MetroMan

Payments

Currency: Chinese Yuan (RMB)

Mobile Payments: Alipay and WeChat Pay are widely used. It’s best to carry cash and international credit cards as well.

ATMs: Available in most areas; check international withdrawal fees with your bank.

Handy Chinese Phrases

Greetings

Hello: 你好 (Nǐ hǎo)

Thank you: 谢谢 (Xièxiè)

Please: 请 (Qǐng)

Excuse me/Sorry: 对不起 (Duìbuqǐ)

Directions

Where is...? ...在哪里? (… zài nǎlǐ?)

I want to go to... 我想去... (Wǒ xiǎng qù…)

Emergency

Help! 救命! (Jiùmìng!)

Police: 警察 (Jǐngchá)

Hospital: 医院 (Yīyuàn)

Local Tips and Etiquette

Dining Etiquette

Use chopsticks, and place them on the chopstick rest when not in use. Join in toasts during meals by saying “Ganbei!” (Cheers).

Gifts

It’s customary to bring a small gift for the host, often something from your home country.

Respect for Elders

Show respect to elders by greeting them first and allowing them to be seated first.
